About PNE Arena

Established in 2005, PNE ARENA has grown to become a trusted name in custom products and school supplies. With nearly 20 years of experience, we remain active in both online and offline markets.

Our official showroom is located in Sungai Petani, Kedah, and in 2025, we're proud to expand further by being available on major online platforms — making it easier than ever for customers to connect with us.

We're committed to delivering quality, trust, and satisfaction to every customer. Thank you for supporting a local brand that's growing strong with you.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality custom products and school supplies that meet the diverse needs of our customers while maintaining affordability and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of custom products and school supplies in Malaysia, known for our quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new and better ways to serve our customers.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Community: We support and contribute to the communities we serve.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How long has PNE Arena been in business?

A: PNE Arena was established in 2005, bringing nearly 20 years of experience in custom products and school supplies.

Q: Where is your showroom located?

A: Our official showroom is located in Bandar Laguna Merbok, Sungai Petani, Kedah.

Q: What are your business hours?

A: We are open Monday to Friday, from 9am to 6pm.

Products & Services

Q: What types of custom products do you offer?

A: We offer a wide range of custom products including convocation robes, t-shirt printing, medals/trophies, towels with embroidery, keychains, nametags, and corporate shirts.

Q: How do I place an order?

A: You can place an order by visiting our product pages, filling in your requirements, and clicking the "Checkout via WhatsApp" button to send your order details directly to us.

Q: Can I see samples before placing a bulk order?

A: Yes, we can provide samples for bulk orders. Please contact us via WhatsApp to discuss sample requests.

Payment & Pricing

Q: How is pricing determined for custom products?

A: Pricing varies based on product type, quantity, customization requirements, and materials used. We provide quotes after reviewing your specific requirements.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, cash payments at our showroom, and online payment options. Details will be provided when you contact us.

Shipping & Delivery

Q: Do you offer shipping?

A: Yes, we offer shipping throughout Malaysia. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location and order size.

Q: How long does it take to complete custom orders?

A: Production time varies depending on the product and order quantity. Typically, custom orders take 7-14 business days to complete. We'll provide a specific timeline when you place your order.

Returns & Exchanges

Q: What is your return policy?

A: Due to the custom nature of our products, we generally do not accept returns or exchanges. However, if there's an issue with your order, please contact us immediately, and we'll work to resolve it.

Q: What if my order is damaged or incorrect?

A: If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours of receipt. We'll arrange for a replacement or correction at no additional cost.

Still Have Questions?

If you didn't find the answer to your question, please don't hesitate to contact us directly. We're here to help!

Shipping Policy

Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Same Day Delivery: Available for Sungai Petani area only (additional charges apply)

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated based on:

  • Order weight and dimensions
  • Delivery location
  • Selected shipping method

Free shipping is available for orders over RM200 within Peninsular Malaysia.

Processing Time

Please note that processing time is separate from shipping time:

  • Standard Products: 1-2 business days
  • Custom Products: 7-14 business days (depending on complexity)

Custom products require additional time for production before they can be shipped.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or WhatsApp. You can use this number to track your package on the courier's website.

Delivery Areas

We ship to all locations within Malaysia, including:

  • Peninsular Malaysia
  • Sabah & Sarawak
  • Labuan

International shipping is not available at this time.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please contact us immediately:

  • Wrong Address: Please double-check your shipping address before confirming your order. We are not responsible for deliveries to incorrect addresses provided by customers.
  • Failed Delivery Attempts: If delivery fails due to customer unavailability, additional charges may apply for redelivery.
  • Lost Packages: In the rare event that your package is lost in transit, we will work with the courier to locate it or arrange for a replacement.

Special Instructions

If you have special delivery instructions, please include them when placing your order. While we'll do our best to accommodate requests, we cannot guarantee that all special instructions will be fulfilled by the courier.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our shipping policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Returns & Exchanges

General Policy

At PNE Arena, we strive to provide high-quality custom products that meet your expectations. However, we understand that sometimes issues may arise. Please review our return and exchange policy below.

Custom Products

Due to the personalized nature of custom products, we generally do not accept returns or exchanges unless:

  • The product is defective or damaged upon arrival
  • There is an error in the customization (e.g., wrong name, spelling mistake) that was made by us
  • The product does not match the specifications provided in your order

Standard Products

For non-customized standard products, we accept returns or exchanges within 7 days of delivery, provided that:

  • The product is unused and in its original condition
  • All original packaging and tags are intact
  • You provide proof of purchase

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

If you believe your order qualifies for a return or exchange, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp at +6013-4036123 within 48 hours of receiving your order
  2. Provide your order number and a clear description of the issue
  3. Include photos or videos showing the problem (if applicable)
  4. Wait for our response and further instructions

Refund Process

If your return is approved, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Exchange Process

For approved exchanges, we will send you a replacement product as soon as possible. In some cases, we may ask you to return the original product first.

Shipping Costs

  • If the return or exchange is due to our error, we will cover all shipping costs
  • If the return or exchange is due to customer error or preference, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs

Exceptions

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Custom products that have been correctly produced according to customer specifications
  • Products that have been used, altered, or damaged by the customer
  • Products returned after the 7-day window
  • Products without proof of purchase

Contact Information

If you have any questions about our return and exchange policy, please contact us:

pnearena@outlook.com

Policy Updates

PNE Arena reserves the right to update this return and exchange policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Privacy Policy

Introduction

At PNE Arena, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Order information and customization details
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring websites

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in order processing and fulfillment
  • With payment processors to complete transactions
  • As required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Secure servers and encryption technologies
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments
  • Employee training on data protection

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint with a supervisory authority

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
