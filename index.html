About PNE Arena

Established in 2005, PNE ARENA has grown to become a trusted name in custom products and school supplies. With nearly 20 years of experience, we remain active in both online and offline markets.

Our official showroom is located in Sungai Petani, Kedah, and in 2025, we're proud to expand further by being available on major online platforms — making it easier than ever for customers to connect with us.

We're committed to delivering quality, trust, and satisfaction to every customer. Thank you for supporting a local brand that's growing strong with you.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality custom products and school supplies that meet the diverse needs of our customers while maintaining affordability and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of custom products and school supplies in Malaysia, known for our quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new and better ways to serve our customers.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Community: We support and contribute to the communities we serve.
